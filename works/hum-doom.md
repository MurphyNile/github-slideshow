# Hum Doom

## Description (EN)

HUM DOOM is an interactive sound installation that stages the stock market as a ritual instrument. The work uses Feng Shui as spatial logic and places it in tension with contemporary financial speculation.

Real-time and historical market data are translated into an unstable sonic system. Volatility is not explained visually; it is experienced as pulse, pressure, interference, and rupture. A custom synthesizer sonifies live trading data from NVIDIA, Intel, and AMD in relation to the S&P 500, while also folding the structural shock of the 2008 global financial crisis into the present tense of trading.

The work is activated through audience participation. Visitors rotate a physical dial using slow circular gestures reminiscent of Tai Chi. Each gesture disturbs the current sound field, detunes the system, and opens a different acoustic route.

Shaped by both the speculative rise and crash of the Shanghai Stock Exchange (2007–2008) and recent public rejection of metaphysical market forecasting in Chinese regulatory discourse, Hum Doom repositions the market as both algorithm and ceremony.

## 项目说明（中文）

HUM DOOM 是一件将股票市场转化为“仪式性乐器”的互动声音装置。作品将风水作为空间逻辑使用，并使其与当代金融投机形成张力。

实时与历史市场数据被转译为不稳定声场。波动不通过图表解释，而以脉冲、压力、干扰与断裂被感知。核心装置是一台定制合成器：它将 NVIDIA、Intel、AMD 的实时交易数据与标普 500 的整体走势进行声化，并把 2008 年全球金融危机的结构性冲击折叠到当下交易时态。

作品通过观众参与被激活。观众以类似太极的缓慢环形动作旋转实体旋钮。每次介入都会扰动既有声场、使系统失谐，并开启新的声学路径。

Hum Doom 同时受到 2007–2008 年上海证券交易所投机性涨跌，以及中国监管语境中对玄学式市场预测公开否定的影响，将市场重置为算法与仪式并存的场域。
