## Description (EN)

HUM DOOM is an interactive sound installation that stages the stock market as a ritual instrument. Rather than treating Feng Shui as an ornamental reference, the project uses it as a spatial logic and places it in direct tension with contemporary financial speculation. Real-time market data and historical financial records are translated into an unstable sonic system, where volatility is not explained or visualized but encountered as pressure, pulse, interference, and rupture.

At the center of the work is a custom-built synthesizer that sonifies live trading activity from NVIDIA, Intel, and AMD in relation to the broader movement of the S&P 500, while folding the structural shock of the 2008 global financial crisis into the present tense of trading. These signals do not produce a linear narrative. They generate recurring surges, feedback, and collapse, evoking hùndùn: not pure randomness, but an unstable order in which information, belief, and capital continue to bind and amplify one another.

HUM DOOM is activated through audience participation. Visitors engage the installation by rotating a physical dial through slow, circular gestures reminiscent of Tai Chi. Each intervention unsettles the existing sound field, detunes the system, and opens a new acoustic path. Listening becomes a bodily negotiation with a predictive machine.

Shaped by both the speculative rise and crash of the Shanghai Stock Exchange from 2007 to 2008 and the recent public rejection of metaphysical market forecasting within Chinese regulatory discourse, Hum Doom reimagines the market as both algorithm and ceremony. As instrument and interface, it makes audible the belief structures inside financial rationality, and the vibrations through which market violence returns to the body.

## 项目说明（中文）

HUM DOOM 是一件将股票市场转化为“仪式性乐器”的互动声音装置。项目并未将风水作为装饰性元素，而是将其作为空间逻辑，并使之与当代金融投机形成直接张力。实时市场数据与历史金融记录被转译为不稳定的声音系统，波动不再被解释或可视化，而是以压力、脉冲、干扰与断裂的形式被身体感知。

作品核心是一台定制合成器：它将 NVIDIA、Intel、AMD 的实时交易活动与标普 500 的整体走势进行声化，并把 2008 年全球金融危机的结构性冲击折叠进当下交易时态。这些信号并不生成线性叙事，而是持续产生峰值、回授与坍塌，指向 hùndùn（混沌）：并非纯粹随机，而是一种信息、信念与资本相互绑定并彼此放大的不稳定秩序。

HUM DOOM 通过观众参与被激活。观众以类似太极的缓慢环形手势旋转物理旋钮，每一次介入都会扰动既有声场、让系统失谐，并开启新的声学路径。聆听在此成为身体与预测机器之间的协商过程。

作品同时受到 2007 至 2008 年上海证券交易所投机性涨跌，以及中国监管语境中对玄学式市场预测公开否定的影响。Hum Doom 将市场重新想象为算法与仪式并置的场域。作为乐器与界面，它使金融理性内部的信念结构变得可听，也使市场暴力如何以振动形式回到身体这一过程得以被感知。
