## Description (EN)

Image Against Oppression is an online audiovisual performance that turns the problem of “sensitive images” into an executable interface condition. Developed from a set of private archives, the work refuses to present clear images as evidence, spectacle, or visual proof to be circulated once again. Instead, through live adversarial degradation, it holds each frame at the threshold of legibility, sustaining a persistent tension between “almost seeing” and “being unable to confirm.” The audience is not asked to consume content, but to become aware of how perception itself has already been trained by the interface.

The work does not treat “sensitivity” as a category of content, but as a form of interface—one that is constantly negotiated between platform policy, imagined collective judgment, and the thresholds of bodily tolerance. Through the real-time modulation of blur, thresholds, temporal rupture, displacement, and the rhythm of black frames, the performance interrupts the seemingly seamless readability of information flow and exposes the operational layer through which images are flagged, looped, restricted, and redistributed under regimes of algorithmic similarity. What emerges is a form of interface consciousness: an awareness that online viewing is never a neutral process, but one entangled with channels of distribution, mechanisms of governance, and the management of affect.

Here, “degradation” is not simply a loss of information, but an ethical and aesthetic strategy. It refuses the interface demand that everything must become identifiable, searchable, and shareable, while preserving the pressure carried by the encounter itself. The work asks how one might watch responsibly within systems that monetize anger, and how platform power quietly orchestrates collective emotion through thresholds of visibility.

## 项目说明（中文）

《Image Against Oppression》是一场在线视听表演，它将“敏感影像”的问题转化为一种可被执行的界面条件。作品基于一组私人档案展开，拒绝将清晰图像作为证据、奇观，或可被再次传播的视觉证明。相反，它通过现场执行的对抗性降质，使每一帧都停留在可辨认性的边缘，在“几乎看见”与“无法确认”之间维持持续的张力。观众在这里并非被要求去消费内容，而是被引向察觉：感知本身如何早已被界面所训练。

作品并不将“敏感性”视为一种内容类别，而是将其理解为一种界面形式——它始终在平台政策、被想象的集体判断，以及身体承受阈值之间被不断协商。通过对模糊、阈值、时间断裂、位移与黑场节奏的实时调制，表演打断了信息流表面上无缝且清晰的可读性，并暴露出影像在算法相似性机制下被警示、循环、设限与再分发的操作层。由此浮现出的，是一种“界面意识”：也即对在线观看从来不是中性过程的认识——它始终与分发路径、治理机制及情感管理相互耦合。

在这里，“降质”并不只是信息的损失，而是一种伦理与美学策略。它拒绝服从界面对一切都必须变得可辨认、可检索、可分享的要求，同时保留遭遇本身所带来的压力。作品所追问的是：在那些将愤怒货币化的系统内部，我们究竟如何才能负责任地观看；以及平台权力又是如何通过可见性的阈值，悄然编排集体情绪。
