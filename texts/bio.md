# Bio

Murphy Nile works across expanded animation, machinima, interactive sound, and installation. Her projects look at how technical systems shape attention, movement, and decision-making through interfaces, metrics, and feedback.

Her work has been shown in exhibitions, screenings, and performance programs in Asia and Europe.

## 简介

Murphy Nile 的创作横跨扩展动画、机械影像、互动声音与装置。她关注技术系统如何通过界面、指标与反馈机制影响注意力、身体移动与决策方式。

她的作品已在亚洲与欧洲的展览、放映与表演项目中呈现。
