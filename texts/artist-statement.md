# Artist Statement

I work with real-time graphics, machinima, sound, and installation. I am interested in systems that appear open, but quietly guide behavior.

For me, the game engine is not only a production tool. It is also a live environment with its own logic: prompts, update cycles, interfaces, and rewards. These elements shape how we move, what we notice, and how choices become habits.

Many of my works build immersive situations that are engaging but hard to step out of. I often focus on small operations such as screen-switching, repeated prompts, and rhythmic feedback. These are simple techniques, but they can direct attention and structure consent.

I use image, sound, and timing to make this tension felt in the body.

## 艺术家陈述

我的创作使用实时图形、机械影像、声音与装置。我关注的是那些看似开放、却在无声中引导行为的系统。

对我来说，游戏引擎不只是生产工具，也是一个具有自身逻辑的实时环境：提示、更新周期、界面与奖赏会共同影响人的移动方式、注意力分配与决策习惯。

我的很多作品会构建一种有吸引力、但不容易退出的沉浸情境。我常处理一些看似微小的操作，例如屏幕切换、重复提示与节奏化反馈。这些技术很简单，却能持续引导注意力并组织“同意”。

我希望通过图像、声音与时间安排，让这种张力被身体直接感受到。
